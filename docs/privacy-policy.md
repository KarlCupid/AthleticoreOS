# AthletiCore OS Privacy Policy

Last updated: `2026-04-08`

## Overview

AthletiCore OS helps athletes manage training, recovery, nutrition, hydration, and fight-prep workflows. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app stores, how it is used, and what controls are available to users.

## Information We Store

AthletiCore OS may store:

- account details used for sign-in
- athlete profile inputs such as age, height, weight targets, and planning preferences
- readiness check-ins, workout history, nutrition logs, hydration logs, and body-weight entries
- planning, scheduling, and fight-camp setup information
- custom exercises, custom foods, and other information you enter directly into the app

## How We Use Information

We use stored information to:

- personalize daily training, readiness, nutrition, hydration, and weight-class guidance
- keep your history available across sessions and devices
- support account access, troubleshooting, and user-requested support
- maintain app functionality and user-requested account actions such as sign-out or deletion

## What We Do Not Claim

AthletiCore OS provides coaching-oriented educational guidance. It does not provide licensed medical care, diagnosis, or emergency services.

## Sharing

The current product experience is designed around delivering app functionality and user support. This policy draft should be updated if additional processors, analytics providers, or sharing flows are introduced.

## Data Retention

User data is retained while an account remains active so the app can provide history-based planning and guidance. If you delete your account inside the app, the app will attempt to remove the associated stored account and app data.

## Your Controls

You can:

- review and update profile inputs in the app
- sign out at any time
- permanently delete your account and associated app data from `Me` > `Delete account`

## Contact

Questions about privacy or account issues can be sent to `support@athleticore.app` or the currently configured support contact for the app.

## Publishing Note

Before App Store submission, publish this policy at a public HTTPS URL and set that URL in App Store Connect and `EXPO_PUBLIC_PRIVACY_POLICY_URL`.
